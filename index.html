Press A Key to Start

Rules:

  • This game tests your memory skills.
  • Therefore you need to remember the color order and click them accordingly from the begining every time a new color is given.
  • For example, in level one random color Red blinks then you need to click the red color.
  • Then you will go to level 2 cause it was correct
  • In level 2 again another random color will blink and assume it was blue.
  • Then this time you need to click red first then click blue. Then you will be taken to level 3
  • Similarly you need to press the buttons from the starting level including the new color button at the present level
  • There are button sounds too so keep your volume high and that will help you too!

Hope you guys have fun!!!
